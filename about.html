About

Nestled on the edge of a pristine, emerald forest and perched above a rushing mountain river, our adventure hotel offers an unparalleled escape for the intrepid traveler. With its rustic charm and modern amenities, this unique retreat serves as a gateway to thrilling outdoor experiences. From white-water rafting and zip-lining through the treetops to guided hikes that reveal hidden waterfalls, the possibilities for adventure are endless. After a day of excitement, guests can unwind in our cozy, well-appointed rooms, savoring delicious local cuisine in our on-site restaurant. Our adventure hotel promises a perfect blend of adrenaline and relaxation in a breathtaking natural setting.
